SDG-7 Affordable & Clean Energy

Universal Access to Energy

Ensure that all people have access to electricity and clean cooking solutions. Approximately 759 million people worldwide lacked access to electricity in 2020.

SDG-7 Affordable & Clean Energy

Increase Renewable Energy Share

Substantially increase the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix by 2030. Transitioning to renewable sources is vital for reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

SDG-7 Affordable & Clean Energy

Improve Energy Efficiency

Double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency by 2030. Enhancing energy efficiency can reduce energy consumption and costs.

SDG-7 Affordable & Clean Energy

Investment in Energy Infrastructure

Facilitate investments in energy infrastructure and clean energy technology. Sustainable energy infrastructure supports economic growth and innovation.

SDG-7 Affordable & Clean Energy

Research and Innovation

Promote research and innovation in energy technology. Technological advancements can lead to more efficient and sustainable energy systems.

SDG-14 Life Below Water

Reduce Marine Pollution

By 2025, prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kinds, particularly from land-based activities, including marine debris and nutrient pollution.

SDG-14 Life Below Water

Protect Marine Ecosystems

By 2020, sustainably manage and protect marine and coastal ecosystems to avoid significant adverse impacts, including strengthening their resilience.

SDG-14 Life Below Water

Minimize Ocean Acidification

Minimize and address the impacts of ocean acidification by enhancing scientific cooperation at all levels and increasing global action.

SDG-14 Life Below Water

Sustainable Fishing

By 2020, effectively regulate harvesting and end overfishing, illegal, unreported, and unregulated fishing to restore fish stocks at least to levels that can produce maximum sustainable yield.

SDG-14 Life Below Water

Conserve Coastal Areas

Conserve at least 10% of coastal and marine areas by 2020, consistent with national and international law, and based on best available scientific information.

SDG-15 Life on Land

Combat Desertification

By 2030, combat desertification, restore degraded land and soil, including land affected by desertification, drought, and floods, and strive to achieve a land degradation-neutral world.

SDG-15 Life on Land

Halt Deforestation

By 2020, promote the implementation of sustainable management of all types of forests, halt deforestation, and restore degraded forests to increase forest coverage.

SDG-15 Life on Land

Protect Biodiversity

Take urgent and significant action to reduce the degradation of natural habitats, halt biodiversity loss, and protect endangered species to prevent extinction.

SDG-15 Life on Land

Promote Sustainable Use of Ecosystems

Ensure the conservation and sustainable use of terrestrial and inland freshwater ecosystems and their services, such as forests, wetlands, mountains, and drylands.

SDG-15 Life on Land

Combat Wildlife Trafficking

Take urgent measures to end poaching and trafficking of protected species, and address both demand and supply of illegal wildlife products.
